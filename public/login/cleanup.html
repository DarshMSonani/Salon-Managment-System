Clean Up

"Revitalize Your Radiance, Unveil Pure Perfection: Face Clean Up, The Maruti X's Signature Glow."

Rejuvenate your skin at Maruti X Salon with our signature Face Clean Up. Let our expert estheticians gently cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate your complexion, leaving you glowing with confidence. Experience the transformative power of our skincare expertise in just one session.


Step Of Maruti X Clean Up:

  • Cleansing: Start by cleansing the face thoroughly to remove any dirt, oil, and makeup residues. This step helps to prepare the skin for further treatments.
  • Steam: Apply steam to the face to open up the pores and soften the skin. This makes it easier to remove impurities and blackheads.
  • Exfoliation: Gently exfoliate the skin using a scrub or exfoliating product to remove dead skin cells and unclog pores. This step helps to reveal smoother, brighter skin.
  • Extraction (Optional): If necessary, perform manual extraction to remove any blackheads or whiteheads. This step requires careful attention to avoid causing damage to the skin.
  • Mask Application: Apply a face mask suitable for your skin type and concerns. Masks can help to further cleanse, hydrate, or treat specific skin issues.
  • Massage: Perform a relaxing facial massage using moisturizer or facial oil. This helps to improve blood circulation, relax facial muscles, and enhance product absorption.
  • Toning: Apply a toner to balance the skin's pH levels and tighten the pores. Toners also help to remove any remaining impurities and prepare the skin for moisturization.
  • Moisturization: Finish by applying a moisturizer suitable for your skin type to hydrate and nourish the skin. Moisturizers help to lock in moisture and maintain the skin's natural barrier.
  • Sun Protection: If the facial clean up is performed during the day, apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen to protect the skin from harmful UV rays.
