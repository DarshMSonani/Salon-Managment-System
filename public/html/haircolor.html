Hair Color

"Transform Your Look: Discover the Perfect Hair Color for a New You"


Elevate your style with a splash of vibrance from Great X. Our expert hair color services bring your desired shade to life, reflecting your personality and flair. From subtle highlights to bold transformations, trust Great X to deliver stunning, long-lasting results. Our skilled professionals use premium products to ensure your hair's health and radiance. Experience the art of color at Great X and embrace a fresh, captivating look that's uniquely you.


Step Of Great X Hair Color:

  • Consultation: Begin with a consultation to discuss your desired color and assess your hair's condition.
  • Preparation: Your hair will be prepped, which might involve lightening or cleaning, depending on the desired color.
  • Mixing: The chosen hair color is mixed according to your preferences and applied strategically.
  • Application: The color is carefully applied to sections of your hair, ensuring even coverage.
  • Processing: Allow the color to process, giving it time to develop and achieve the desired hue.
  • Rinse and Condition: After processing, your hair is rinsed and conditioned to lock in the color and enhance shine.
  • Style: Once dried, style your newly colored hair to showcase your stunning transformation.
