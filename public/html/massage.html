Massage

"Relax, Recharge, Renew: Let Our Massage Melt Your Stress Away."


Indulge in relaxation at Maruti X Salon with our expert massages. From Swedish to deep tissue, our skilled therapists tailor each session to melt away stress and leave you feeling renewed. Experience the ultimate self-care and emerge revitalized and ready to shine.


Step Of Maruti X Massage:

  • Consultation: Begin with a consultation to discuss any specific concerns or preferences with your massage therapist.
  • Preparation: Prepare the massage area by ensuring the room is warm, dimly lit, and free from distractions. Your therapist may also offer you privacy to undress to your comfort level.
  • Choice of Oil: Your therapist will offer a selection of massage oils or lotions for you to choose from, or they may have a preferred option based on your preferences and needs.
  • Starting Position: You'll typically start lying face down on a massage table, covered with a sheet or towel. Your therapist will ensure you're comfortable before beginning.
  • Technique: Your therapist will use a variety of techniques such as effleurage (long, sweeping strokes), kneading, friction, and percussion to manipulate your muscles and promote relaxation.
  • Communication: Throughout the massage, communicate with your therapist about the pressure, areas of focus, and any discomfort you may experience. They can adjust their technique accordingly.
  • Turning Over: Midway through the session, you may be asked to turn over so that the therapist can work on the front of your body.
  • Completion: At the end of the session, your therapist will gently signal that the massage is coming to an end. Take your time getting up from the table, and your therapist may offer you water to help rehydrate.
  • Post-Massage Care: Your therapist may provide recommendations for post-massage care, such as staying hydrated, avoiding strenuous activity, and scheduling follow-up appointments if desired.
